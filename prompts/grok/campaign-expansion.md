# Grok Prompt Pack - Campaign Expansion

## Purpose

Use these prompts to generate bold ideas, campaign angles, positioning variants, and fast market-facing concepts.

## Prompt 1 - Campaign Angles

Generate 20 campaign angles for `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com`, a business website that combines business growth, funding pathways, AI services, automation systems, and future-ready business support.

Include a mix of:
- serious professional angles
- futuristic angles
- startup angles
- established business owner angles
- ecosystem / network angles

## Prompt 2 - Headline Variants

Generate 30 headline variations for a homepage that helps people build, fund, automate, and expand their businesses with AI-assisted services and connected systems.

## Prompt 3 - Offer Expansion

Take this core offer and expand it into:
- 10 sub-offers
- 10 lead magnet ideas
- 10 social post ideas
- 10 CTA ideas
- 5 partnership ideas

Keep the ideas practical enough to implement and broad enough to support future growth.
