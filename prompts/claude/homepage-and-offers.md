# Claude Prompt Pack - Homepage and Offers

## Purpose

Use these prompts to generate clear, persuasive, and structured website content.

## Prompt 1 - Homepage Copy

Create homepage copy for `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com`, a business website that helps startups and established business owners with business funding pathways, AI services, automation systems, strategy support, and connected ecosystem opportunities.

Write:
- hero headline
- subheadline
- primary CTA text
- secondary CTA text
- short section explaining the Business of Businesses concept
- 6 service bullets
- short trust-building section
- FAQ with 6 questions

Tone:
- confident
- forward-looking
- practical
- flexible
- not overhyped

## Prompt 2 - Offer Page Builder

Create a service page for one offer category from this list:
- business funding help
- AI business setup
- automation services
- strategy and planning
- ecosystem participation

For the chosen category, write:
- headline
- intro
- who it is for
- benefits
- process
- CTA section
- FAQ
