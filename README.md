# lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-

The public business website and integration hub for the **lippytm AI business funding ecosystem**.

This repository is intended to become the front door for a broader **Business of Businesses** model that connects:
- business funding pathways
- AI products and services
- chatbot systems
- automation workflows
- educational and entertainment ecosystems
- affiliate and partnership opportunities
- future CRM, payment, and lead-routing systems

## Core Purpose

This website is being developed as a flexible growth platform for:
- startups
- solo founders
- affiliate builders
- service businesses
- established business owners
- AI-enabled agency models
- future educational and automation ecosystems

The main public-facing mission is to help visitors:
- understand offers quickly
- enter the right funnel
- connect with AI support systems
- request help with business growth and funding
- move into follow-up, automation, and conversion workflows

## Ecosystem Connections

This repository is designed to connect with:
- **MyClaw** for workflow automation, intake, routing, and operational flows
- **Claude** for long-form copy, structured writing, proposal generation, and knowledge support
- **Grok** for ideation, angle generation, campaign variation, and creative exploration
- **lippytm.ai** and related repositories for shared systems, brand expansion, and future orchestration
- future integrations such as ManyChat, BotBuilders, Facebook, Instagram, lead forms, email systems, and payment gateways

## Strategic Positioning

This project is not only a website. It is intended to become:
- a marketing platform
- a conversion engine
- an integration switchboard
- a content distribution hub
- an automation intake layer
- a foundation for a scalable AI-assisted business network
