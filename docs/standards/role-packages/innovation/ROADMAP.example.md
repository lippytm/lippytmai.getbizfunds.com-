# ROADMAP

## Purpose
Describe the expected evolution of this innovation repository.

## Strategic Direction
This repository is evolving into a clearer experiment layer that tests ideas quickly and makes useful lessons easier to move into stronger long-term repos.

## Current Stage
- draft

## Near-Term Priorities
### 0 to 30 Days
- improve experiment labeling
- improve lesson capture
- improve visibility of promising ideas

### 30 to 90 Days
- improve movement of validated ideas into product or knowledge repos
- improve reuse of successful prompts and concepts
- improve clarity around experiment maturity

### 90+ Days
- support more structured innovation paths
- improve idea-to-product promotion
- reduce stranded experiments

## Capability Growth Plan
| Capability | Current State | Target State | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prompting | medium | strong | often central to experiments |
| Knowledge | medium | stronger | promote lessons learned |
| Analytics | low | medium | improve test visibility |
| Governance | low | medium | improve experiment clarity |

## Integration Growth Plan
- improve signals from front-door and product repos
- improve handoff to knowledge repos
- improve visibility to control_tower repos
- reduce stranded experiments
