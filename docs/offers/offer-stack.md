# Offer Stack

## Purpose

Define a flexible offer structure that can grow from simple service offers into a broader AI-assisted business ecosystem.

## Core Offer Categories

### 1. Business Funding Help
Possible positioning:
- business funding guidance
- funding readiness support
- business growth pathways
- resource navigation
- offer packaging support

### 2. AI Business Setup
Possible positioning:
- AI-assisted business systems
- chatbot setup
- website-to-workflow automation
- operations support
- content system setup

### 3. Automation Services
Possible positioning:
- workflow building
- intake automation
- customer follow-up systems
- lead routing
- simple operational dashboards

### 4. Strategy and Planning
Possible positioning:
- startup planning
- offer architecture
- funnel planning
- AI opportunity mapping
- ecosystem growth strategy

### 5. Affiliate / Expansion Layer
Possible positioning:
- partnership opportunities
- referral systems
- educational ecosystems
- resell or ecosystem participation models

## Good Offer Ladder

### Entry Level
- free guide
- checklist
- discovery form
- chatbot interaction
- short consultation request

### Mid Level
- setup session
- mini audit
- basic automation build
- starter AI business package

### Higher Level
- full buildout
- multi-step funnel system
- automation plus content support
- strategic ecosystem implementation

## Best Practices

- keep offers understandable in plain language
- separate visitor education from technical delivery details
- give users a quick-win entry path
- keep high-ticket offers tied to clear outcomes
- build landing pages for major offer categories instead of mixing everything on one page

## Main CTA Candidates

- Get Funding Help
- Start My AI Setup
- Build My Automation System
- Book a Strategy Session
- Explore the Ecosystem
