# AI CRM Lifecycle Review Checklist

## Purpose
Use this checklist when reviewing CRM lifecycle quality, continuity, engagement, satisfaction, and upgrade readiness.

## Intake and Qualification
- [ ] source is visible
- [ ] primary intent is visible
- [ ] desired outcome is visible
- [ ] urgency is visible
- [ ] next best action exists

## Lifecycle Continuity
- [ ] lifecycle stage is current
- [ ] ownership is visible
- [ ] last meaningful touch is visible
- [ ] next step timing is visible
- [ ] weak handoff points are documented if relevant

## Engagement Quality
- [ ] first interaction was clear enough
- [ ] routing seems appropriate
- [ ] support or follow-up continuity is preserved
- [ ] summary quality is strong enough for human handoff

## Satisfaction and Risk
- [ ] satisfaction signal is visible when relevant
- [ ] churn risk is visible when relevant
- [ ] unresolved friction is not hidden
- [ ] recovery path exists when needed

## Growth and Reuse
- [ ] repeat-value potential is visible
- [ ] referral or loyalty potential is visible
- [ ] education or journey opportunity is visible when relevant

## Final Review Outcome
- [ ] healthy
- [ ] needs cleanup
- [ ] at risk

## Best Practices
- keep lifecycle reviews practical and repeatable
- use this checklist to improve next actions, not just record status
- connect lifecycle quality to customer and client experience quality
