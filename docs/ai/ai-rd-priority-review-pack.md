# AI R&D Priority Review Pack

## Purpose
Use this pack to help AI systems review research and development priorities across the wider ecosystem.

## Review Questions
- Which experiments are closest to durable value?
- Which ideas have the highest reuse potential?
- Which R&D areas have the strongest customer or client impact?
- Which efforts are under-documented?
- Which efforts should be paused, promoted, or strengthened?

## Suggested Prompt Ideas
- ai-rd-leverage-review-v1
- ai-rd-reuse-potential-review-v1
- ai-rd-documentation-gap-review-v1
- ai-rd-promotion-readiness-review-v1
- ai-rd-priority-sequencing-review-v1

## Best Practices
- prioritize R&D that improves clarity, reuse, and customer experience
- promote durable lessons into stronger canonical homes
- avoid letting speculative work crowd out high-leverage operational improvements
- use structured review outputs so R&D priorities can be compared over time
