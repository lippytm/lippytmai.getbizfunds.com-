# AI Customer Trust and Clarity Review Pack

## Purpose

This pack defines how AI systems and humans can review customer and client trust, clarity, confidence, and perceived continuity across first touch, onboarding, delivery, support, and retention.

## Core Principle

Trust grows when people can understand:
- what is happening
- what happens next
- who is responsible
- what to expect
- whether the system remembers their context

## Review Areas

### 1. Clarity at Entry
Review:
- value clarity
- route clarity
- next-step clarity
- confusion signals

### 2. Confidence During Service
Review:
- milestone visibility
- progress transparency
- summary usefulness
- dependency clarity

### 3. Trust During Support
Review:
- issue understanding
- corrective action clarity
- ownership clarity
- follow-up quality

### 4. Trust During Retention and Expansion
Review:
- whether relationship value is visible
- whether timing feels appropriate
- whether trust is strong enough for renewal, referral, or upsell asks

## Best Practices
- improve clarity before persuasion
- improve continuity before asking for more commitment
- use trust review to strengthen both CRM and repo-level systems
- keep trust signals visible enough for early intervention
