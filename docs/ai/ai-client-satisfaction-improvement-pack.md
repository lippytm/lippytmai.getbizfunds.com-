# AI Client Satisfaction Improvement Pack

## Purpose

This pack defines how AI systems can help monitor, improve, and protect client satisfaction across the lifecycle.

## Satisfaction Improvement Areas
- onboarding clarity
- communication consistency
- milestone confidence
- support responsiveness
- issue recovery quality
- retention readiness
- referral readiness

## AI Satisfaction Review Questions
- Does the client know what is happening next?
- Are milestone updates visible enough?
- Is there unresolved friction?
- Are churn or trust risks visible?
- Is the best next action supporting confidence or creating confusion?

## Suggested Prompt Ideas
- ai-client-satisfaction-review-v1
- ai-client-confidence-review-v1
- ai-client-friction-review-v1
- ai-client-churn-risk-review-v1
- ai-client-referral-readiness-review-v1

## Best Practices
- review satisfaction at milestone moments
- distinguish delivery issues from communication issues
- use AI to surface risk earlier, not later
- connect satisfaction review to practical recovery or growth actions
