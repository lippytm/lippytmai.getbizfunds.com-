# AI Innovation Governance Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook defines how innovation, experimentation, and exploratory AI work can stay creative while still remaining understandable, promotable, and safe enough for ecosystem reuse.

## Core Principle

Innovation should remain flexible, but not invisible.
Experiments should be easy to scan, compare, promote, or retire.

## Governance Areas

### 1. Experiment Clarity
Review:
- what is being tested
- why it matters
- what success looks like
- current maturity

### 2. Documentation Quality
Review:
- experiment summaries
- findings
- promotion decisions
- next actions

### 3. Promotion Readiness
Review:
- what should stay experimental
- what should move into knowledge repos
- what should move into product or workflow repos
- what should be retired

### 4. Learning Visibility
Review:
- whether useful lessons are easy to find
- whether lessons are stranded in local notes
- whether experiment results are reusable enough

## Best Practices
- keep innovation flexible but reviewable
- use templates to improve comparability
- promote durable value into stronger long-term repos
- retire unclear or low-value experiments instead of letting them drift forever
- connect innovation governance to learning promotion and asset reuse
