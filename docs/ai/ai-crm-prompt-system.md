# AI CRM Prompt System

## Purpose
This document defines a reusable AI-generated prompt system for CRM, customer lifecycle operations, support quality, satisfaction, retention, and growth across the wider ecosystem.

## Core Principle
AI CRM prompts should help humans and systems:
- capture cleaner records
- improve routing decisions
- improve lifecycle continuity
- detect risk earlier
- improve satisfaction
- improve reuse of customer intelligence
- improve next-best-action quality

## Main CRM AI Prompt Domains

### Domain 1 — Intake and Qualification
Prompts that help classify need, urgency, service fit, and best next step.

### Domain 2 — Lifecycle Hygiene
Prompts that detect missing fields, stale records, weak next actions, and unclear ownership.

### Domain 3 — Satisfaction and Churn Risk
Prompts that detect dissatisfaction, confusion, unresolved friction, and churn signals.

### Domain 4 — Expansion and Reuse
Prompts that detect upsell, cross-sell, referral, education, and partner opportunities.

### Domain 5 — Support and Recovery
Prompts that improve triage, summarize issues, and help plan recovery.

### Domain 6 — CRM Governance
Prompts that review data quality, stage discipline, and asset consistency.

## Best Practices
- keep CRM prompts structured and action-oriented
- use prompts to improve continuity, not just create more text
- make next action and ownership visible
- use CRM prompts at milestone moments, not only at intake
