# AI CRM Satisfaction and Loyalty KPI Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook defines how AI systems and humans can use CRM satisfaction and loyalty KPIs to improve relationship continuity, trust, retention, repeat value, and advocacy.

## Core Principle

Satisfaction and loyalty metrics should help teams act earlier and more intelligently.
They should not be passive numbers with no next action.

## KPI Families

### 1. Satisfaction Visibility
Use KPIs such as:
- satisfaction_signal_strength
- milestone_confidence_score
- support_resolution_confidence

Why it matters:
- makes hidden dissatisfaction easier to detect
- improves client confidence
- strengthens recovery timing

### 2. Continuity and Trust
Use KPIs such as:
- next_action_visibility_score
- handoff_context_quality
- trust_risk_visibility

Why it matters:
- reduces repeated explanation
- improves confidence in the process
- protects relationship quality

### 3. Retention Readiness
Use KPIs such as:
- churn_risk_visibility
- renewal_readiness_signal
- relationship_health_score

Why it matters:
- supports timely retention actions
- reduces silent drop-off
- improves loyalty planning

### 4. Advocacy and Growth Readiness
Use KPIs such as:
- referral_readiness_signal
- repeat_value_potential
- education_or_journey_readiness

Why it matters:
- improves timing for advocacy asks
- supports higher-value continuation paths
- avoids premature expansion pressure

## Review Rhythm

### Weekly
Focus on:
- churn risk visibility
- stale satisfaction signals
- weak next actions
- unresolved support confidence gaps

### Monthly
Focus on:
- renewal readiness
- referral readiness
- trust continuity
- relationship health trends

### Quarterly
Focus on:
- whether KPI definitions are helping real decisions
- whether satisfaction review is improving retention outcomes
- whether loyalty and advocacy actions are timed well

## Best Practices
- connect every KPI family to a practical next action
- improve relationship clarity before expansion asks
- use AI to surface weak continuity and trust signals earlier
- keep KPI use grounded in customer and client experience improvement

## Strategic Outcome

This playbook helps the ecosystem use satisfaction and loyalty KPIs to improve trust, continuity, retention, and long-term relationship value across CRM operations.
