# AI Retention and Loyalty Prompt Pack

## Purpose
Use these prompts to help AI systems improve retention, loyalty, renewals, repeat engagement, and relationship continuity.

## Prompts

### 1. ai-retention-risk-review-v1
Review this customer or client record and return:
1. strongest retention signal
2. strongest churn signal
3. weakest continuity point
4. best next retention action
5. whether human outreach should happen now

### 2. ai-loyalty-growth-review-v1
Review this relationship and return:
1. strongest loyalty indicator
2. strongest repeat-value opportunity
3. best education, support, or upgrade path
4. best next action to deepen trust without pressure

### 3. ai-renewal-readiness-review-v1
Review this active or recently completed service relationship and return:
1. readiness for renewal
2. missing confidence factors
3. best renewal-supporting action
4. best signal that the relationship is not ready yet

## Best Practices
- improve continuity before asking for expansion
- use AI to spot risk and loyalty patterns earlier
- connect retention actions to visible value, not just sales pressure
