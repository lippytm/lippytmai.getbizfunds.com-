# AI Customer Experience Improvement Pack

## Purpose

This pack defines how AI systems can improve customer and client experience across the ecosystem.

## Main CX Improvement Areas
- intake clarity
- faster routing
- better next steps
- better support continuity
- lower confusion
- better status visibility
- better recovery handling
- stronger satisfaction follow-up

## AI CX Review Questions
- Is the first interaction clear?
- Is the next step obvious?
- Is relationship context preserved?
- Are support handoffs too weak?
- Are satisfaction signals visible enough?
- Are unresolved issues being detected early enough?

## AI CX Improvement Prompt Ideas
- ai-cx-first-touch-review-v1
- ai-cx-next-step-clarity-review-v1
- ai-cx-support-continuity-review-v1
- ai-cx-status-visibility-review-v1
- ai-cx-friction-reduction-review-v1

## Best Practices
- improve clarity before adding cleverness
- preserve context through handoffs
- reduce uncertainty with visible next actions
- use AI to shorten confusion, not lengthen it
