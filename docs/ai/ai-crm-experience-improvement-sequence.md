# AI CRM Experience Improvement Sequence

## Purpose

This guide defines a practical order for improving CRM experience quality across intake, continuity, support, satisfaction, retention, and loyalty.

## Sequence

### Step 1 — Improve First-Touch Clarity
Make source, intent, first next action, and preferred interaction mode more visible.

### Step 2 — Improve Lifecycle Continuity
Make stage freshness, next-best action, owner, and timing more visible.

### Step 3 — Improve Handoff Quality
Strengthen summaries, dependency notes, and context preservation.

### Step 4 — Improve Satisfaction Visibility
Make satisfaction signals, friction, and churn risk easier to detect.

### Step 5 — Improve Support and Recovery
Strengthen issue summaries, recovery plans, and trust repair actions.

### Step 6 — Improve Retention and Loyalty Timing
Use renewal, repeat-value, and referral readiness more carefully and visibly.

### Step 7 — Improve Reuse and Learning
Promote durable CRM patterns into guides, templates, and registries.

## Best Practices
- improve clarity before automation complexity
- improve continuity before expansion asks
- improve visibility of risk before visibility of growth opportunities
- promote durable CRM patterns into canonical reusable assets
