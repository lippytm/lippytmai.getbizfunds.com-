# AI Customer Journey Diagnostics Pack

## Purpose

This pack helps AI systems diagnose customer and client journey quality across first touch, qualification, onboarding, delivery, support, satisfaction, retention, and advocacy.

## Core Principle

A healthier customer journey is not only about conversion.
It is about preserving clarity, continuity, confidence, and useful next steps across the whole lifecycle.

## Diagnostic Journey Stages

### Stage 1 — First Touch
Questions:
- Is the first interaction clear?
- Is the value understandable quickly?
- Is the next step visible?

### Stage 2 — Qualification
Questions:
- Is the user’s need captured clearly?
- Is urgency visible?
- Is the handoff path appropriate?

### Stage 3 — Onboarding
Questions:
- Are expectations clear?
- Is the first milestone visible?
- Is the user likely to feel supported?

### Stage 4 — Active Delivery
Questions:
- Is progress visible?
- Is context preserved?
- Is the client likely to feel momentum?

### Stage 5 — Support and Recovery
Questions:
- Are issues summarized clearly?
- Is corrective action obvious?
- Is trust at risk?

### Stage 6 — Satisfaction and Retention
Questions:
- Are satisfaction signals visible?
- Is churn risk visible?
- Is the next relationship-strengthening action clear?

### Stage 7 — Advocacy and Expansion
Questions:
- Is the relationship strong enough for referral, renewal, or expansion?
- Is the ask appropriately timed?
- Is the value path obvious?

## Best Practices
- diagnose journey friction by stage, not only overall sentiment
- improve continuity before expansion
- use journey diagnostics to improve both CRM and repo-level assets
- keep the best next action visible at every stage
