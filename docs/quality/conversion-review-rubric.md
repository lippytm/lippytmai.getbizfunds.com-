# Conversion Review Rubric

This rubric helps review changes to `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-` for clarity, routing quality, and business value.

## Review Dimensions
- offer clarity
- funnel fit
- form clarity
- routing quality
- handoff quality
- service activation fit
- public trust impact

## Best Practices
- keep value practical and easy to understand
- review form friction against offer value
- review premium paths for clarity
- keep payment logic out of public pages
