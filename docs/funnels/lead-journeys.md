# Lead Journeys

This document maps how different visitors should move through `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-` from first contact to qualification, follow-up, and next action.

---

## Funnel Design Objective

The site should support multiple journey types without becoming confusing.

The job of the funnel system is to:

- qualify interest quickly
- route the visitor to the right offer path
- reduce drop-off
- support automation and future CRM flows
- enable both fast action and higher-touch guidance

---

## Core Journey Types

### 1. Funding Journey
Entry signals:
- wants funding help
- needs growth capital guidance
- wants help organizing next steps

Suggested path:
1. hero CTA
2. funding page
3. funding intake form
4. qualification review
5. follow-up sequence
6. strategy or service handoff

### 2. AI Systems Journey
Entry signals:
- wants AI tools for business
- wants chatbot or AI workflow help
- wants productivity or systems upgrades

Suggested path:
1. hero CTA or AI systems page
2. AI business systems explanation
3. AI intake form
4. routing to setup or strategy path
5. follow-up nurture or session booking

### 3. Automation Journey
Entry signals:
- wants more automation
- has repetitive work to reduce
- needs routing, follow-up, or content systems

Suggested path:
1. automation page
2. automation outcomes explanation
3. automation-focused intake
4. qualification and categorization
5. premium offer or strategy handoff

### 4. Strategy Journey
Entry signals:
- wants expert help quickly
- prefers custom direction
- has a more complex business case

Suggested path:
1. strategy session CTA
2. credibility / positioning section
3. strategy intake form
4. confirmation and scheduling path
5. premium buildout opportunity

### 5. Affiliate / Partner Journey
Entry signals:
- interested in partnering
- interested in promoting ecosystem offers
- interested in white-label or growth collaboration

Suggested path:
1. affiliate or partner CTA
2. ecosystem explanation
3. partner qualification form
4. review and onboarding sequence
5. future commerce or reward system connection

---

## Journey Branching Logic

Each lead journey should classify the visitor by:

- offer interest
- business stage
- urgency
- complexity
- willingness to book a call
- whether they need education first or action first

---

## Best Practices

- present no more than a few primary CTAs on major pages
- use intent-based pages, not random generic pages
- route unclear visitors to guidance rather than forcing a hard sell
- separate educational nurture flows from ready-to-act flows
- keep form friction proportional to offer value

---

## Suggested Conversion Events

- CTA clicked
- form started
- form submitted
- strategy interest marked
- premium path selected
- partner path selected
- follow-up sequence started

These events should later feed automation and reporting.

---

## Follow-Up Patterns

### Quick Qualification Follow-Up
For ready leads.

### Nurture Follow-Up
For curious but not yet ready leads.

### Premium Follow-Up
For complex or higher-value opportunities.

### Partner Follow-Up
For affiliate, collaborator, or reseller interest.

---

## Rule of thumb

A strong lead journey does not force everyone into the same funnel. It helps each visitor move through the right doorway, at the right speed, with the right next step.
