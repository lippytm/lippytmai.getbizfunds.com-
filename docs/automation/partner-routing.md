# Partner Routing

This document explains how partner and affiliate opportunities should be routed in `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-`.

## Goal

Move partner and affiliate interest into clearer next steps without mixing those paths into standard customer flows.

## Routing Triggers
- partner inquiry form submitted
- affiliate interest signaled
- reseller or collaboration fit identified
- partner-origin lead quality appears strong

## Routing Inputs
- contact_id
- primary_goal
- partner_type
- urgency
- related offer or service
- next_action

## Best Practices
- keep partner paths separate from standard customer intake when useful
- preserve source and partner context during handoff
- route partner opportunities based on real fit and repeat value
- use partner-origin outcomes to improve offer clarity and support flows
