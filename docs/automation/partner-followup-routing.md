# Partner Followup Routing

This document explains how partner and affiliate-origin contacts should move through follow-up paths after the first routing event.

## Goal

Keep partner and affiliate follow-up timely, source-aware, and aligned to the next best business step.

## Routing Triggers
- partner-origin lead needs follow-up
- partner-origin premium path needs clarification
- activation completed and next step is needed
- repeat engagement suggests a stronger fit

## Routing Inputs
- contact_id
- source_partner_id
- lead_stage
- related offer or package
- next_action
- service_state

## Best Practices
- preserve partner source context through follow-up
- keep partner follow-up distinct when it improves reporting or routing quality
- route high-fit partner contacts into premium or service paths quickly
- use follow-up quality as a partner-channel performance signal
