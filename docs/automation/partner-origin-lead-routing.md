# Partner-Origin Lead Routing

This document explains how leads that come from partner or affiliate paths should be routed in `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-`.

## Goal

Preserve source context so partner-origin leads can move through the right qualification, premium, or service paths.

## Routing Inputs
- contact_id
- source_partner_id
- related offer
- lead_stage
- urgency
- next_action

## Best Practices
- keep partner-origin context attached through routing
- separate partner-origin reporting from general lead reporting when useful
- review partner-origin lead quality before scaling partner traffic
- route high-fit partner leads into the right premium or service path quickly
