# Lead to Swarm Routing

This document explains how qualified leads from `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-` can be routed into assistant, swarm, strategy, or premium paths.

## Routing Pattern
1. lead enters through page or form
2. lead is classified by goal and urgency
3. simple cases route to form follow-up or assistant path
4. more complex cases route to swarm or premium review paths

## Best Practices
- keep routing rules simple enough to audit
- preserve lead context during handoff
- route complex cases upward rather than forcing generic flows
