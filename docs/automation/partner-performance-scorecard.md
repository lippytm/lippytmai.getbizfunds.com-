# Partner Performance Scorecard

This document defines a simple scorecard for reviewing partner and affiliate channel quality in `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-`.

## Scorecard Areas
- partner-origin lead quality
- premium-path fit
- checkout and activation quality
- follow-up quality
- repeat engagement or expansion quality

## Best Practices
- score quality before scaling volume
- review activation and repeat value, not only top-of-funnel counts
- compare partner-origin paths against standard paths when useful
- use low scores to trigger bounded improvement missions
