## Phase 5 - Growth Layer

- landing pages by audience type
- lead magnets
- email capture sequences
- affiliate and partnership flows
- social content routing

## Operational Best Practices

- document decisions in the repo
- keep prompts versioned
- create reusable page templates
- separate brand messaging from technical notes
- use one shared source of truth for offers and positioning
- keep integrations modular so systems can be swapped later
