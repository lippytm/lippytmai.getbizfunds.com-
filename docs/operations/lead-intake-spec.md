# Lead Intake Specification

## Goal

Capture enough information to route the visitor correctly without making the form feel heavy.

## Recommended Primary Form Fields

### Essential
- Full name
- Email
- Business name
- What do you need help with?
- Short description of current need

### Suggested Dropdown Categories
- Business Funding Help
- AI Business Setup
- Automation Services
- Strategy and Planning
- Affiliate / Partnership Interest
- General Question

### Optional Fields
- Phone number
- Website or social link
- Business stage
- Best time to contact

## Business Stage Options
- Idea stage
- Startup stage
- Active business
- Growing business
- Established business

## Form Copy Guidance

Use simple copy such as:

"Tell us a little about what you need so we can help route you in the right direction."

## Routing Logic

### Business Funding Help
Route to funding-pathway workflow and follow-up questions around goals, stage, and readiness.

### AI Business Setup
Route to AI setup workflow and questions around use case, tools, and goals.

### Automation Services
Route to workflow planning questions and current process pain points.

### Strategy and Planning
Route to discovery and prioritization questions.

### Affiliate / Partnership Interest
Route to ecosystem and collaboration flow.

### General Question
Route to manual review or general support workflow.

## Best Practices

- keep first-touch forms short
- ask deeper questions later in the workflow
- make routing categories easy to understand
- avoid technical jargon in public forms
- design follow-up questions by category rather than using one long universal form
